Getting Started

Leave Planner helps you find the best opportunities to maximize your time off by combining holidays with minimal leave days.

How to Use

Importing Your Holidays

You can import your holiday calendar in three ways:

  • Scan Calendar: Use your camera to scan your calendar. The app will automatically extract holiday dates.
  • Upload Screenshot: Upload a screenshot of your calendar. The app will process it to find holidays.
  • Import CSV: Import a CSV file with your holidays. The file should have columns for date and holiday name.

Viewing Leave Opportunities

Once your holidays are imported, the app automatically calculates the best leave opportunities. You'll see:

  • How many days off you'll get
  • How many leave days you need to take
  • A calendar view showing the dates
  • A detailed breakdown of each day

Settings

Customize your leave preferences:

  • Preferred Leave Day: Choose Friday, Monday, or both to optimize your leave strategy.
  • Reset Data: Clear all your data and start fresh if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app calculate leave opportunities?

The app analyzes your holidays and finds opportunities where you can combine weekends and holidays with minimal leave days to get maximum time off.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

Can I export my data?

Currently, the app focuses on importing holiday data. All calculations and opportunities are displayed within the app.

What if the CSV import doesn't work?

Make sure your CSV file has a "DATE" column and a "DESCRIPTION" column. The date format should be readable (e.g., "1-Jan-2026" or "01/01/2026").

Why aren't my leave opportunities showing?

Make sure you've imported holidays for the current year. The app shows opportunities for the next 6 months from today. Check the Calendar tab to see all opportunities throughout the year.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try resetting the app data from Settings. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app.

Calendar scan not working

Make sure you've granted camera permissions. Ensure the calendar image is clear and well-lit. Try using the screenshot upload option instead.

CSV import errors

Check that your CSV file has proper headers (DATE and DESCRIPTION columns). Ensure dates are in a readable format. Remove any special characters or formatting issues.
