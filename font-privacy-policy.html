Privacy Policy for That's My Font

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Introduction

That's My Font ("we," "our," or "us") operates the That's My Font mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using the That's My Font App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Automatically Collected

We do NOT collect any personal information or user data. The App operates entirely on your device.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Drawn Characters: Your hand-drawn characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9, and optional characters) stored as drawing data
  • Character Adjustments: Position, scale, and alignment settings for each character
  • App Preferences: Drawing tool settings (pen/pencil, brush width, smoothness toggle)

This information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:

  • Generate your custom font from your drawings
  • Remember your character adjustments and preferences
  • Export TTF font files via iOS Share sheet
  • Provide a consistent user experience across app sessions

Data Storage and Security

  • All character drawings and preferences are stored locally on your iPad
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • Your font data remains under your control on your device
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Font Export

When you export a font as a TTF file:

  • The font file is generated locally on your device
  • You choose where to save or share the font using iOS Share sheet
  • Font files can be saved to Files app, shared via AirDrop, or sent via email
  • We do not have access to exported font files
  • You have full control over where and how you share your fonts

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

The App uses Apple's native iOS Share sheet for font export, which is a system feature and does not transmit data to us.

Permissions

iOS Permissions

The App does not request special permissions beyond standard app functionality. The App is designed exclusively for iPad with Apple Pencil support.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as the App is installed on your device
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the App
  • No data is retained on external servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored drawings and preferences through the App
  • Modify or delete your drawings at any time
  • Export your fonts as TTF files
  • Completely remove all data by uninstalling the App

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Data Transfer

We do not transfer any data outside your device. All processing occurs locally on your iPad.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use analytics or tracking services. The App does not collect any usage data or analytics.

International Users
