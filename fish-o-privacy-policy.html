Privacy Policy for Fish-o Hydrate

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Fish-o Hydrate ("we," "our," or "us") operates Fish-o Hydrate for iOS and Apple Watch (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personal information or analytics data. The app operates entirely on your device.

  • The app stores your water intake records and goals locally on your device. No usage data or personal information is sent to any server.
  • If you grant permission, the app will use Apple HealthKit to read and write water intake information. This data remains within your Apple HealthKit account and is not shared externally.
  • All hydration records, goals, and settings are securely stored on your device and, if enabled, within your private iCloud or HealthKit environment for Apple Health sync.

Apple HealthKit

  • The App only accesses HealthKit with your explicit consent.
  • No HealthKit data is ever transmitted to us, shared externally, or used for advertising or analytics.
  • HealthKit permissions can be managed at any time from your device settings.

Permissions

  • HealthKit: Used to read and write water intake data (if you grant access).
  • Notifications: Used only to remind you to drink water at your chosen interval. No notification content leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone, including children. The App is suitable for all ages, as it does not collect any personal data.

Security

  • All data is stored securely on your device using standard Apple security practices.
  • Your data is never transmitted to any server or shared with any third party.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately. Please review this policy periodically for updates.
