Introduction

Time Progress Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Time Progress Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using the Time Progress Tracker App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Automatically Collected

We do NOT collect any personal information or user data. The App operates entirely on your device.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • User Preferences: Your chosen perspective (Half Full/Half Empty)
  • Time Format Settings: Your preferred time mode (24 Hours/Office Hours)
  • App Settings: Configuration preferences you select

This information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:

  • Remember your app preferences across sessions
  • Provide personalized time tracking views based on your chosen perspective
  • Maintain your selected time format settings
  • Ensure a consistent user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All user preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • Your data remains under your control on your device
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Android Only)

Our Android app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process certain information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Expo Framework

The App is built using Expo, a React Native development framework. Expo does not collect personal data from end users of our App. For development purposes only, anonymous crash reports may be collected to improve app stability.

Permissions

Android Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Required for audio-related features (if enabled in future versions)
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: Required to control audio settings (if enabled in future versions)

Note: Currently, the Time Progress Tracker app does not actively use these permissions for its core time tracking functionality. These permissions are included for potential future features.

iOS Permissions

The App does not request special permissions beyond standard app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Widget Functionality

The App includes native home screen widgets for iOS and Android:

  • Widget Data: Widgets display time progress information based on your device's current date and time
  • Local Processing: All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • Auto-Updates: Widgets refresh automatically every hour using local time data
  • No External Communication: Widgets do not transmit any data externally

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as the App is installed on your device
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the App
  • No data is retained on external servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored preferences through the App settings
  • Modify or delete your preferences at any time through the App interface
  • Completely remove all data by uninstalling the App

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
