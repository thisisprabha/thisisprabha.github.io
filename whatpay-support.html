Getting Started

Whaypay helps you track and manage all your subscriptions, payments, and EMIs in one place. Get a clear view of your monthly and yearly expenses, and never miss a payment.

How to Use

Adding a Subscription or EMI

  • Tap the "ADD NEW" button at the bottom of the screen
  • Enter the subscription/EMI details:
    • Title (e.g., "Netflix", "Internet Bill")
    • Price amount
    • Billing cycle (Monthly, Yearly, or Weekly)
    • Payment type (Subscription or EMI)
    • Next payment date
    • Category (Entertainment, Health, Utility, or Other)
  • For EMIs, specify the total duration and how many payments you've completed
  • Tap to save your subscription

Viewing Your Expenses

  • The main screen shows your total monthly expenses at the top
  • Tap the total amount to cycle through different views:
    • Monthly: Total monthly cost of all subscriptions
    • Yearly cost: Total yearly cost of all subscriptions
    • Yet to pay: Amount due in the current month
  • Each subscription card shows the price and next payment date

Expanding a Card

  • Tap any subscription card to expand it and see detailed information
  • Expanded view shows:
    • Yearly cost for that subscription
    • Amount yet to pay this month
    • Next payment date
    • For EMIs: Remaining EMI count and total amount
  • Tap outside the card or scroll to collapse it

Editing or Deleting

  • Expand a card to see the edit and delete buttons at the bottom
  • Tap the pencil icon to edit subscription details
  • Tap the trash icon to delete a subscription

Filtering by Category

  • Use the filter button (bottom left) to view subscriptions by category
  • Select a category to see only those subscriptions
  • Select "All" to view all subscriptions again

Exporting Your Data

  • Triple-tap the total amount at the top to export your data as a CSV file
  • The CSV file includes all subscription details and can be opened in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets

Widget

  • Add the Whaypay widget to your home screen to see your total expenses at a glance
  • Long press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, and search for "Whaypay"

Settings

  • Edit Your Name: Tap on your name at the top of the screen to personalize it (defaults to "My")
  • Upcoming Payments: The app automatically shows a scrolling marquee of payments due within 3 days

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app calculate yearly cost?

The app converts all subscriptions to yearly amounts:

  • Monthly subscriptions: Price × 12
  • Yearly subscriptions: Price as is
  • Weekly subscriptions: Price × 52

What is "Yet to pay"?

"Yet to pay" shows the total amount of subscriptions that have a payment due in the current month. This helps you see how much you need to pay this month.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers. Your data is only shared with the widget extension on your device.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Triple-tap the total amount at the top of the screen to export all your subscription data as a CSV file. You can then share it or open it in spreadsheet apps.

How do I track EMIs?

When adding a new subscription, select "EMI" as the payment type. Enter the total EMI duration (e.g., 12 months) and update the "Completed EMIs" count as you make payments. The app will show you how many EMIs remain and the total amount left to pay.

What happens if I delete the app?

All your data will be deleted from your device. Make sure to export your data before deleting if you want to keep a backup.

Why don't I see the widget?

Make sure you've added at least one subscription. The widget needs data to display. Also, ensure you've granted the necessary permissions when adding the widget to your home screen.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, restart your device. As a last resort, you can delete and reinstall the app (note: this will delete all your data unless you export it first).

Data not saving

Make sure you tap the save button after entering subscription details. If data still doesn't save, try restarting the app or your device.

Widget not updating

Widgets may take a few moments to update. Try removing and re-adding the widget to your home screen. Make sure the app has the necessary permissions.

Export not working

Make sure you have at least one subscription added. Triple-tap the total amount (not just tap once). If it still doesn't work, try restarting the app.

Can't edit or delete subscriptions

Make sure you've expanded the card first by tapping on it. The edit and delete buttons appear at the bottom of the expanded card view.
