The real problem

Announcing isn't the hard part. Placement is.

Your release notes live outside the product — in an email, a changelog, a banner people scroll past. The feature lives somewhere else entirely. So the one moment a user could act on the news is the one moment the message isn't there.

Put the explanation next to the change, at the moment it matters, and the thing you shipped finally gets found.

Why FeaturePing

Two kinds of tools exist. Neither does this job.

Changelog widgets · Beamer, Canny, AnnounceKit

Adoption platforms · Pendo, WalkMe, Appcues

Powerful, heavy, pricey
