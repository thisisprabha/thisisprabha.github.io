How Prabha + Tina Work

One page. No jargon. No cron. Just the daily flow.

🔄 The Daily Flow

1
You drop notes into Obsidian raw/ folder or message Tina on Telegram. Anytime during the day
2
Wiki absorbs them. Nothing is auto-created. Notes just sit in archive files (tasks.md, links.md, synthesis.md). Every evening around 6 PM
3
Tina reads synthesis and sends you a suggestion list: "Found 3 items — add to Todoist?" You trigger this by messaging me, or I notice raw files
4
You pick and approve. I add tasks to the right board with the right label. Whenever you reply
5
Execute when you say go. Research and Tina tasks run instantly. Design and app builds wait for your green light. Anytime you message "execute X"

📋 The 4 Boards

Tina Board
Tina owns this

Self-improvement, setup fixes, error log research, LLM issues. Tina auto-manages these. You don't touch.

Professional
Needs approval

SuperOps design work, Arun feedback, form layouts, AI kit. Research runs auto. Design waits for your go.

App Works
Needs approval

WhatPay, ASC submissions, iOS builds. Planning runs auto. Actual build/release waits for your go.

Personal
Empty for now

Blog, HTML artifacts, Hyperframes — dormant until you decide what goes here.

🏷️ Task Labels = Who Does What

No labels = ready to execute. Other labels gate the work.

Auto Tina runs these immediately. Research, fixes, setup.
Needs Approval You must say "go" before Tina touches design/code.
Needs Details You edit the task description first. Then approve.
No Label Task is ready. Execute on your command.

🛡️ Core Rules

Tina never auto-creates tasks. You approve every addition to Todoist. No surprises.
Tina never executes your work tasks without approval. Design, code, builds = your gate.
Email is dead for tasks. No digests. No reply-to-approve. Todoist is the single queue.
You can add tasks directly too. Drop a task in Telegram → I add to board instantly. Paste into Todoist app yourself anytime.
Deleted tasks stay deleted. If you delete from Todoist, Tina ignores it. Cancelled = cancelled.
