Privacy Policy for Sip Up!

Last Updated: December 06, 2025

Introduction

Prabhakaran ("we," "our," or "us") provides the Sip Up! app, a gamified water tracking application designed to help you build a healthy hydration habit through a virtual aquarium experience. The app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This policy informs you regarding our collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

Sip Up! is designed with your privacy in mind. We minimize the data we collect.

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

To provide you with comprehensive hydration tracking, Sip Up! may request access to read and write water intake data to Apple Health (HealthKit).

  • How we use it: We use this data solely to display your hydration progress within the app and to update your Apple Health records when you log water in Sip Up!.
  • Storage: This data remains on your device and in your secure iCloud Health store. We do not transmit your health data to any external servers or third parties.

Local Data Storage

Your game progress (aquarium status, fish unlocked, coins, etc.) and app settings (reminders, preferences) are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage technologies (UserDefaults and CoreData). This ensures your aquarium is available even when offline.

Log Data

In the unlikely event of an app crash, we may collect anonymous Log Data through standard operating system reporting tools. This may include your device model, OS version, and the time of the crash, solely for the purpose of fixing bugs and improving stability.

Notifications

Sip Up! uses local notifications to send you hydration reminders ("Time to sip!" or "Save your fish!"). These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use remote push servers to track your notification habits.
