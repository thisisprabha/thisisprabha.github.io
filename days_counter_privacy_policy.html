Privacy Policy for Days counter

Last Updated: December 06, 2025

Introduction

Prabhakaran ("we," "our," or "us") provides the Days counter app, a time tracking utility designed to help you visualize your yearly progress and count down to important events. The app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This policy informs you regarding our collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We respect your privacy. Days counter does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers.

Local Data Storage

All the data you enter into the app—including your custom events (names and dates), display preferences, and settings—is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure UserDefaults and App Group technologies.

  • Purpose: This local storage allows the app and its widgets to function seamlessly and remember your preferences.
  • Security: This data never leaves your device (except when you perform a standard iCloud backup of your device). We do not have access to your events or personal schedule.

Log Data

In the case of an error in the app, we may collect anonymous data and information (through standard Apple crash reporting tools) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device model, operating system version, and the time of the crash. This is used solely to fix bugs and improve app stability.

Service Providers

The app does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you.

Children’s Privacy

Days counter is a general utility app safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents can be assured that their child's usage data remains private.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
