How It Works

1. Stay Hydrated, Keep Fish Alive

Drink water to hit your daily goal. Your hydration directly impacts your aquarium's health. Consistent hydration keeps your water clean and your fish happy!

2. Water Quality Indicator

The water in your tank reflects your consistency:

  • Clean Blue Water: You are meeting your goals. Nice job!
  • Yellow Water: Warning! You missed yesterday's goal. Drink water today to clean it up.
  • Brown Water (Fish Death): Danger! You missed your goal for 2 consecutive days. Your fish will die, and you will need to reset the tank to start over.

3. Weekly Unlocks & Progress

Consistency pays off! Unlock new items for your aquarium every week by hitting your streaks:

  • New Fishes: Unlock exotic species like Snow, Woody, Miho, Candy, Dante, and Wall-E.
  • Decorations: Add colorful sand, pebbles, and ornaments.
  • Plants: Grow a lush underwater garden with new plant unlocks.

App Features

Smart Notifications

Get intelligent reminders between 9:00 AM and 10:00 PM (or your configured wake/sleep times). The app reminds you only when needed to help you stay on track without being annoying.

Calm Meditation Mode

Need a break? Open the app to watch your fish swim peacefully. Enjoy the calming underwater visuals to relax and de-stress.

Cross-Device Support
