Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Introduction

Finger Doodles (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simple drawing app for kids and anyone who enjoys creating with their fingers. We designed it to protect your privacy and keep data on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on our servers. The app keeps your content on your device only:

  • Drawings and Placed Parts: Your artwork stays locally on your device.
  • Colors, styles, and settings: Saved locally to improve your experience.

Photos and Files

If you export or share a drawing, the image is generated on your device. You choose where to send or save it. We do not see or receive these files.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. There are no ads, analytics, or tracking SDKs in the app.

Kids’ Privacy

Finger Doodles is kid-friendly. We do not collect personal information from children. Parents and guardians should supervise sharing/exporting drawings if desired.

Storage and Security

All app data remains on your device. Removing the app will delete its stored data. Use your device’s backup (e.g., iCloud) if you want to preserve drawings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last Updated” date when we do. Please review this policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Reach out through the app’s support channel or your preferred contact method.

Your Consent

By using Finger Doodles, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
