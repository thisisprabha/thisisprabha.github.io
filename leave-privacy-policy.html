Privacy Policy

Introduction

Whaypay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Whaypay is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Subscription and Payment Data: Information about your subscriptions, EMIs, payment amounts, billing cycles, and payment dates that you manually enter into the app.
  • User Name: The name you choose to display in the app (defaults to "My" if not set).

Data Storage

All data you enter in Whaypay is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults and App Groups. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Accessible only to you and the app's widget extension

Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, or share your data with any third parties. Your data remains on your device at all times. The only exception is:

  • Widget Extension: Your subscription data is shared with the Whaypay widget extension through Apple's App Groups feature, allowing the widget to display your information. This sharing occurs entirely on your device.

Data Export

You can export your subscription data as a CSV file using the app's export feature. This export is performed locally on your device, and you choose where to share the exported file.

Third-Party Services

Whaypay does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate with any external data collection services.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual subscriptions or all data at any time through the app. When you delete the app from your device, all associated data is removed.

Children's Privacy

Whaypay is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels or your preferred method of communication.

Your Consent

By using Whaypay, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
